Cookies & Cream Ice Cream

While this will be to make cookies and cream ice cream. The base of it can be used to make whatever you'd like for add ins.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 Cup sugar
  • 1 Cup Whole milk
  • 2 Cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 tsp guar gum
  • 10 Double Stuf Oreos chopped

Instructions

  1. In a Mixer, mix eggs until they lighten in color, then slowly add the sugar.
  2. Lower mixer speed and slowly add the milk and cream.
  3. Sprinkle in guar gum and mix for a few minutes.
  4. Now that your base has been made start your ice cream maker and follow instructions on how to use it.

    5. *I use a KitchenAid ice cream maker so I add in the oreos as it's mixing, yours may need to just be added to the base.
