Chocolate Chip Cookie Mug Cake

This is such a great snack to make when your sweet tooth takes over. Quick and easy with minimal mess for when you don't want to put in much effort.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Tbsp butter
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 drops of vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/4 Cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/8 tsp baking soda
  • 1 Tbsp milk
  • 2 Tbsp semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Melt butter in the mug with a microwave for 40 seconds.
  2. Add sugar, vanilla, and salt and stir with a fork to combine.
  3. Add flour, baking soda, and milk and stir to combine.
  4. Mix in chocolate chips.
  5. Place in microwave for 40-50 seconds and enjoy.
