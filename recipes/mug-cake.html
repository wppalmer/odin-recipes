Funfetti Mug Cake

Description

This is a recipe found on BudgetBytes, which is a great source to find cheap ways to cook I would use this website in college to help me out and keep my cooking skills in check. Anyways back to the mug cake. Who doesn't like a quick fix for that sweet tooth? This should be the perfect way get what you need with little mess, and no worries about leftovers going stale.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp rainbow sprinkles
  • 3 Tbsp milk
  • 1 Tbsp butter (melted)
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Add the flour, sugar, baking powder, and sprinkles to a microwave safe mug (8 oz. or larger). Stir until everything is well combined.
  2. Add the milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract to the mug. Stir until soft batter forms, making sure to scrape all the flour out of the corners of the mug.
  3. Microwave the mug cake for about 60 seconds, or until it has puffed to about 2-3 times the volume. Cook time will vary with wattage of microwave.
  4. Allow the mug to cool for a few minutes and then top with your favorite frosting if desired.
