Veggie Quesadilla

Decription

I discovered this recipe while I was studying abroad in the UK. I quickly learned 2 things while I was there:

  1. "Mexican" food is not good there, and it is too expensive.
  2. If I cut down on meat, I could save more money for nights out or travel.
This was a great recipe for me, as it was cheap to make, and very filling. One of these quesadillas was enough for dinner, and there would be plenty of leftovers for dinner the next day, or for a southwest omelette.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup of sliced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic (chopped)
  • 1 16 oz can of black or kidney beans (drained and mashed)
  • 1/2 cup of spinich
  • 1/2 cup of mushrooms
  • 1 1/2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 1/2 cup of salsa
  • Tortillas
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. In a large pan, fry garlic and onions with knob of butter on medium high heat. Then add mushrooms, fry for a minute, and add beans, corn, and salsa. Salt to taste. Remove mixture into seperate container when heated.
  2. Clean pan, and return to stove. Lower heat to medium.
    Then build quesadilla:
    1. Tortilla
    2. Cheese
    3. Mixture
    4. Spinich
    5. Cheese
    6. Tortilla
  3. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side.
  4. Repeat as desired.
