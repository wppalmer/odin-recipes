Veggie Quesadillas

Now I know what you might be thinking, "How difficult can it be to make a quesadilla?" And you'd be right, it's not difficult. I'm just merely sharing what ingredient combinations I like when I am making a nice and filling veggie quesadilla.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • 1/2 Cup of Onions (Diced)
  • 1 Clove of Garlic(Minced)
  • 16oz Can of Black Beans (Drained & Mashed)
  • 1/2 - 1 Cup of Mushrooms
  • Frozen Corn
  • Jar of Salsa (Your Choice)
  • Baby Spinach
  • Chiuahua Cheese (Or Any Cheese That Melts Well)

Instructions

  1. In a large pan, sautee onions and garlic with butter or olive oil on medium high heat.
  2. Add in mushrooms for about a minute before adding and mixing in beans, corn, and salsa.
  3. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Remove from pan into a seperate container.
  5. Clean pan and return to stove under medium heat.
  6. Build quesadilla with tortillas, cheese, filling, and spinach. Cook until both sides are browned and cheese has melted.
  7. Repeat for how ever many quesadillas you wish to make, or until filling has been used up.
